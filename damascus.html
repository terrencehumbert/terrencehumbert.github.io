  • How to make Damascus

Step 1: Pick your steel

I use 1095 and 15N20 because they have a simlar heat treat but also etch at different rates. 1080 and 15N20 is will work too.

Step 2: Prep your steel

Cut your steel to length and clean off the pickling. I like to cut mine to 4 inches in length. I use my belt grinder to remove the pickling. Once clean stack your steel in alternating layers.

Step 3: Weld your billet together

Use a welder to secure your metal pieces together. Take extra time to ensure there are no gaps between the steel.

Step 4: Heat and Flux

Place the steel in your forge. Once it starts turning a dull red its time to flux. After applying flux bring up the steel to around 2300F. The flux starts to rapid boil at that tempature

Step 5: Squish

Once at temp its time to forge weld. Use your hammer or press to set the welds. Once the welds are set you can continue drawing out the billet.
